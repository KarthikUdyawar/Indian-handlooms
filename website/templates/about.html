HANDLOOMS IN INDIA

Handloom products in India are finely woven texture, where the exchange of structure and example, theme, weaving and painting or other compatible changes and blends, makes every item novel, everything as particular as the following. So what is the handloom weave? A handloom is a straightforward machine utilized for weaving.The types of Handloom products include handloom decorative, handloom fabric, handloom saree, handloom textiles, cotton clothes and more.

The diversity and variation of weaving techniques styles and designs have innumerable art forms and school of practise which make it fairly Complex industry.We bring together an in-depth understanding and comprehensive overview of each of the platforms and leverage our expertise to cater to the precise needs and requirements of the clients.

While the Indian Handloom industry is a highly fragmented and unorganised sector, there are about 5 million recorded weavers in this market of which more than 95% are terminal Weavers. This makes the ecosystem fairly Complex and challenging for someone who wishes to manufacture or procure the right kind of textile at the right price. We bring to table an end-to-end expertise across the entire Handloom value chain, thereby offering a simplified interface between our clients and the vast population of Weavers and artisans.

We help our clients achieve all of those while enabling them to become more sustainable and responsible and nimble in terms of their operations since consumers are becoming more conscious of the brand that they choose are adopting the norms and protocols of Sustainable fashion. Hence we must offer the right kind of solutions to our clients to help them become carbon positive.
